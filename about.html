About Coding Club

A student-led organization dedicated to fostering a love for programming and building a strong community of developers

Our Mission

To create an inclusive environment where students can learn, practice, and excel in programming. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application through hands-on workshops, collaborative projects, and peer-to-peer learning.

Our Vision

To be the premier student coding community that empowers the next generation of developers and innovators. We envision a future where every member has the skills, confidence, and network to succeed in the tech industry.

Our Values

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a small group of passionate computer science students, Coding Club started as weekly meetups in a small classroom. What began with just 10 members has grown into a thriving community of over 250 active developers.

Over the years, we've organized numerous hackathons, workshops, and study groups. Our members have gone on to intern at top tech companies, contribute to major open-source projects, and even start their own tech ventures.

Today, Coding Club continues to be a place where beginners find their footing, intermediate developers sharpen their skills, and experienced coders give back to the community. We're proud of how far we've come and excited about where we're going.
